EcoNex GP

AI for Green Procurement—Powered by EcoNex

The next-generation digital platform for sustainable construction procurement, delivering transparency, data-driven decision-making, and measurable carbon impact across your entire supply chain.

Mission

Transform the construction sector by unifying data, digitizing procurement, and enabling low-carbon choices for a resilient built environment in the Middle East and Africa.

Vision

A future where every project and every supplier is empowered to achieve ambitious sustainability targets—seamlessly, collaboratively, and with accountability.

Approach

Integrate AI, verified carbon data, and unified standards to drive green procurement at every stage—concept, design, tender, construction, and lifecycle management.

Platform Highlights

Unified Data Flow

End-to-end integration from land acquisition and design to procurement and facility management.

AI-Driven Insights

Intelligent recommendations for material selection, carbon footprint, and cost optimization.

Green Standards Ready

Streamlined compliance with LEED, local, and global green procurement standards.

Supply Chain Transparency

Verified, real-time tracking of suppliers’ carbon data, EPDs, and project lifecycle impacts.
